The Hosting Market's Value Chasm

The web hosting industry is built on a fundamental conflict: the promise of low-cost entry versus the reality of high long-term value. This report's analysis reveals a market ripe for disruption, where customer trust is the most valuable and vulnerable asset.

The Price Cliff: A Model Built on Churn

The dominant business model preys on customer inertia, offering tantalizingly low introductory prices that skyrocket upon renewal.[4, 5, 6] This widespread practice creates a continuous cycle of customer frustration and churn, representing a critical vulnerability for incumbents.[7, 8]
